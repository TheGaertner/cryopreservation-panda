23 ## Requirements
24 
25 - NUC with Ubuntu 18.04
26 - Franka Emika Panda connected to the NUC

137 ### Camera Calibration
138 
139 Before using the camera, they have to be calibrated:
140 
141 - Inside the gui choose the *Misc* tab.
142 - Click *Create ChArUco Board* and print it with scale 100%.
143 - Fix the printed paper on a solid surface.
144 - Point the left camera on the board and click *Take Photo*
145 - Change the position of the camera and click again *Take Photo*
146 - Repeat this step 10 times
147 - Now click on Calibrate. The actual calibration parameters are stored in *config/camera_parameters.yaml*

155 ### Process Description
156 
157 Following the required steps are listed:
158 
159 Part 0:
160 - Detect freezer
161 - Detect handling position 0
162 - Detect handling position 1
163 - Detect water bath
164 
165 Part 1:
166 - Open freezer
167 - Open compartment
168 - Grab correct box
169 - Put box in handling position
170 - Close compartment
171 - Close freezer
172 
173 Part 2:
174 - Open box
175 - Pick correct vessel
176 - Put it into holder
177 - Open water bath
178 - Put holder into water bath
179 - Close Water bath
180 
181 Part 3:
182 - Close box
183 - Execute Part 1 (put box back into freezer)
184 
185 Part 4:
186 - Open water bath
187 - Pull holder out of water bath
188 - Close water bath
